The Met Gala, formally called the Costume Institute Benefit and also known as the Met Ball, is an annual fundraising gala for the benefit of the Metropolitan Museum of Art's Costume Institute in New York City. It is one of the most notable fashion events in the world, attracting celebrities, designers, and fashion industry insiders.
